WHAT IS SPOOFING?

Spoofing is when a trader places big fake orders to trick other traders into thinking prices are moving — then cancels those orders and profits from the chaos they created.

1Trader places a massive sell order — price starts to drop as others react
2Other traders see the big order and start selling too
3The cheat cancels their sell order before it goes through
4Price is now lower — the cheat buys cheap
5Repeat the trick. Make money illegally.

The giveaway: big orders that always get cancelled, followed by a small trade going the other way.

A related trick is layering — doing the same fake order over and over to keep pushing the price. Regulators treat layering as a form of spoofing. Both are illegal.
